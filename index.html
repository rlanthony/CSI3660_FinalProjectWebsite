What is a Plex Server?

Plex is a media streaming service that can be accessed through a browser or locally downloaded application. It allows users to stream movies and TV shows, along with listening to music. Users can also share the content with family and friends to have access to the same media. Plex offers a variety of free media to all users, but users can also upload their own to the site to watch from anywhere.

In this project, we have implemented this service by creating a Plex Server on our GCP VM to monitor and control user access to our own server. It provides users with a way to have access to the same media to stream on a server we are monitoring to ensure users have high speed service to the platform.
